## Usage Guidelines

1. **A/B Testing**: Test different taglines to see which performs better
2. **Target Audience**: 
   - Privacy-focused users: Emphasize "Private" and "Offline"
   - Technical users: Mention "ONNX" and "Custom Models"
   - General users: Focus on "Easy" and "Beautiful"
3. **Length**: Keep taglines under 50 characters for better readability
4. **Consistency**: Use consistent messaging across all assets
5. **Emojis**: Use sparingly (maximum 1-2 per tagline)

## Localization Notes

When translating to other languages:
- Keep taglines short and punchy
- Maintain brand voice (privacy-first, tech-forward)
- Ensure keywords translate well
- Test readability at different sizes
