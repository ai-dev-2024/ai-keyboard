How It Works

Three simple steps to AI-powered typing

01

Model Downloader

Download and install ASR models directly in the app. Choose from recommended models or import your own ONNX/Vosk models.

02

Local Inference Engine

All speech recognition happens on-device using ONNX Runtime or Vosk. No internet required, no data leaves your phone.

03

Keyboard Integration
